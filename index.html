Prakriti


Prakriti, the flagship event organized by the Rotaract Club, unfolds in multiple phases, combining a grand plantation drive and captivating animal visits. This unique initiative not only nurtures the environment through extensive tree planting but also fosters a deeper connection with nature by allowing participants to interact with diverse wildlife. Prakriti stands as a testament to the Rotaract Club's commitment to environmental conservation and community engagement, creating a harmonious blend of sustainability and education in each phase of this impactful event.

Sakhi ( S.H.E )


Sakhi (S.H.E.), the flagship project spearheaded by the Rotaract Club DPSRU, unfolds in transformative phases dedicated to sanitary pad distribution and menstrual hygiene. This impactful initiative aims to break barriers surrounding menstruation, ensuring access to essential hygiene products. Through strategic planning and community engagement, Sakhi addresses a crucial aspect of women's health while promoting awareness and empowerment. The Rotaract Club DPSRU's commitment to social welfare shines through Sakhi, as it paves the way for a healthier and more informed community. A total of 2700+ sanitary pads are distributed till now

Sanskriti : A Cross border Fiesta


Sanskriti, the cross-border fiesta, takes center stage as the flagship event of the Rotaract Club DPSRU. This innovative initiative transcends geographical boundaries, bringing together diverse clubs from around the world to represent and celebrate their cultures through an engaging online platform. As a global showcase of unity and diversity, Sanskriti fosters international understanding and collaboration. The Rotaract Club DPSRU's commitment to cultural exchange and mutual respect shines brightly in this dynamic event, creating a vibrant tapestry of traditions and connections.

Arogya


Arogya, the flagship project of DPSRU Rotaract Club, stands as a beacon of health and compassion. With a mission to prioritize community well-being, Arogya focuses on providing free medical check-ups to ensure access to essential healthcare services. This initiative not only addresses immediate health concerns but also promotes preventive care and health awareness. The DPSRU Rotaract Club's commitment to fostering a healthier community is exemplified through Arogya, where the power of compassion and service converge to make a meaningful impact on lives.

Ekagrata


Ekagrata, the flagship project of Rotaract Club DPSRU, is a transformative initiative aimed at enhancing workplace wellness through yoga. This unique project brings the benefits of yoga directly to various workplaces, promoting physical and mental well-being. Ekagrata underscores the Rotaract Club DPSRU's dedication to fostering a harmonious work environment, where individuals can experience the rejuvenating effects of yoga. By seamlessly integrating this ancient practice into modern workplaces, Ekagrata exemplifies the club's commitment to holistic health and a balanced lifestyle.

Karuna : A wardrobe of warmth


Karuna, the flagship project of RAC DPSRU, is a heartwarming initiative dedicated to providing comfort to the less fortunate through "A Wardrobe of Warmth." This benevolent project focuses on collecting and donating blankets to those in need. As the chilly winds of winter approach, Karuna stands as a beacon of compassion, bringing warmth to the lives of the underprivileged. The Rotaract Club DPSRU's commitment to social welfare is beautifully exemplified through Karuna, a gesture that extends a helping hand and spreads the comforting embrace of kindness

Deepotsav


The November Deepotsav at DPSRU was a joyous convergence of faculty, Rotaractors, and students. Engaging games like Tambola and a sweet guessing challenge enlivened the atmosphere. Cultural expressions flourished through singing performances and heartfelt poetry recitations, showcasing diverse talents. Beyond festivities, blankets and sweets were graciously shared with our invaluable cleaning staff and guards, a gesture of heartfelt appreciation. The evening culminated in a beautiful gathering where everyone lit diyas, symbolizing unity and togetherness within our DPSRU family. This event encapsulated our shared values of camaraderie, gratitude, and inclusivity, fostering a stronger bond among our university community.

Annapurna


Annapurna, the flagship project of RAC DPSRU, unfolds as a compassionate endeavor to combat hunger through food donation drives and Langar Sewa for the less fortunate. This initiative symbolizes the Rotaract Club DPSRU's commitment to addressing food insecurity and promoting the spirit of community service. Annapurna strives to make a meaningful impact by ensuring that no one goes to bed hungry, embodying the essence of selfless giving and solidarity in the fight against hunger and poverty.

Manzil-e-Agaz


Manzil-e-Agaz, the installation ceremony of RAC DPSRU for the year 2023, marks a promising commencement towards a year of impactful service and leadership. This event signifies the handover of responsibilities and the induction of new leaders who will steer the club's initiatives. Manzil-e-Agaz encapsulates the spirit of new beginnings, unity, and the shared commitment of the Rotaract Club DPSRU to continue making a positive difference in the community. The ceremony stands as a beacon, guiding the club towards a year of purposeful service.

Kilkari


Kilkari, the flagship project of RAC DPSRU, resonates with the laughter of children through its heartfelt initiative—Rakhi Donation Drive for orphans. This thoughtful project brings joy to the lives of those often overlooked, ensuring that every child, especially orphans, feels the warmth of community care. Kilkari exemplifies the Rotaract Club DPSRU's commitment to spreading happiness and fostering a sense of belonging. Through the Rakhi Donation Drive, the club creates enduring memories and bridges gaps, making a significant impact on the lives of these precious souls

Kalam


Kalam, the flagship project of RAC DPSRU, is an inspiring initiative focused on the power of education through stationary distribution. This project aims to provide essential school supplies to underserved communities, ensuring that every child has the tools to pursue their academic journey. Kalam reflects the Rotaract Club DPSRU's dedication to empowering through education, making a tangible impact by breaking down barriers to learning. Through this thoughtful endeavor, Kalam paves the way for a brighter future, one pencil and notebook at a time.

Uddan


Uddan, the flagship project by RAC DPSRU, soars high on creativity and inclusivity through its vibrant initiative—an annual painting competition for underprivileged children. This uplifting project provides a canvas for young minds to express themselves, fostering artistic talents and instilling a sense of accomplishment. Uddan embodies the Rotaract Club DPSRU's commitment to nurturing the potential of every child, transcending socioeconomic barriers. Through the colors of imagination, Uddan paints a brighter future, encouraging dreams to take flight in the hearts of these young artists.
